Our Story
✦

A Cedar Grove Tradition, Sixteen Years Strong

Tucked away on Pompton Avenue in Cedar Grove, Kyoto Sushi III has been serving some of the finest Japanese food in New Jersey since 2008. What started as a simple passion for quality fish and honest cooking has grown into one of the most beloved spots in Essex County.

Our chefs hand-select the freshest fish each morning and craft every roll, nigiri, and sashimi plate with real care. From our crowd-favorite Pink Lady Roll to full hibachi dinners, every plate reflects a genuine love for Japanese food, served in a warm and welcoming space that keeps guests coming back.
